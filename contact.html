Frequently Asked Questions

  • Formula Student is an international motorsport competition where university teams design, build, and race small single-seater formula-style cars. The competition emphasizes engineering skills, innovation, and teamwork, preparing students for careers in the automotive and motorsport industries.

  • We welcome passionate students from all fields of study to join our team! Recruitment typically takes place at the beginning of the academic year. Keep an eye on our social media channels or website for announcements about upcoming recruitment drives.

  • Our team is entirely funded through sponsorships and partnerships with companies and organizations. We are proud to have a diverse range of sponsors who share our vision and passion for engineering and motorsport.

  • Our goals for the next season include improving the performance of our race car, participating in international competitions, and securing top positions in various categories. We're also focusing on enhancing our team's engineering skills and expanding our network of sponsors and supporters.

  • If you're interested in supporting our team, please visit the Sponsors & Partners section of our website for more details on how to get involved. We offer a range of sponsorship packages and are happy to discuss customized partnerships to align with your goals.
